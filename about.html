Our Mission

● At lingodingo, our mission is to connect language learners with the resources they need to integrate themselves successfully into the language(s) they are learning.
● We believe that when learning a language, we should not just be learning formal speech and grammar but also learn its popular culture and references.
● Learning a language is a gateway to connecting with other people; understanding different cultures is the key to an interconnected world.
● And with a world that feels like it is becoming increasingly smaller with the rise in technology, it’s become more important than ever that we learn to understand those from all around the world.
● Through our platform, language learners are able to assimilate themselves into the current and all-time popular songs, books, movies, and even recipes(coming soon)!
